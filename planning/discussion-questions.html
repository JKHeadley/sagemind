Priority & Focus

Growth & Investment

Q14. Is Gregg a potential investor, advisor, or connector?

He offered to help secure customers or resources. What's the right ask? - Introductions to construction companies? - Help refining the pitch for VC conversations? - Direct investment? - Just ongoing advisory/sounding board?

Given that the main constraint is Justin's time (not capital), would VC money actually help? Or would it create pressure to scale faster than Justin can deliver?

Q16. What's the 12-month vision?

Pick the one that resonates most: - (A) Boutique consulting firm — 5-8 construction clients, high-touch, premium pricing, Justin goes full-time - (B) Product company — Productize the methodology, hire/train others to deliver, Justin focuses on sales and product - (C) Platform play — Build reusable AI tools (booking system, website factory, agent infrastructure), license or white-label - (D) Hybrid — Consulting pays the bills while building toward a product

The answer shapes everything: pricing, hiring, investment, and what to build next.
