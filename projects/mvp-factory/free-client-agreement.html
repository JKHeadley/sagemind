Here's the deal in plain English: Sagemind AI is building you a custom website at no charge — a $1,999 value — as part of our Portfolio Program. In return, you agree to provide a testimonial about your experience and give us permission to feature your site as an example of our work. That's it. No monthly fees, no strings, no surprises. You own your website completely.
1
What Sagemind Will Build For You
  • Custom 5-page website (Homepage, Services, About, Contact, FAQ) — designed around your business
  • Mobile-responsive design — looks great on phones, tablets, and desktops
  • AI chatbot setup, trained on your business information
  • SEO foundation — Schema markup, meta tags, and sitemap so Google can find you
  • Google Maps integration, click-to-call button, and contact form
  • 2 rounds of revisions based on your feedback
  • Domain setup and deployment assistance — we'll walk you through it
  • Complete source code and documentation delivered at handoff
  • Permanent backup of your website kept on our end
2
What You Agree To
  • Provide a written or video testimonial within 30 days of your site going live
  • Allow Sagemind to display your website as a portfolio example on sagemindai.io and in marketing materials
  • Allow Sagemind to mention your business name and logo in portfolio-related marketing
  • Deliver all required content (photos, business info, copy, logos) within 30 days of signing this agreement
  • Set up and maintain your own accounts for domain registration, hosting, and the AI chatbot API (see Section 5 for cost estimates)
3
Ownership & Rights
  • You own your website. The source code, design, and all content created specifically for you belong to you — fully and permanently.
  • Portfolio rights. Sagemind retains the right to showcase the completed website in our portfolio and marketing materials.
  • Removal option. Either party may request removal of the portfolio display with 30 days' written notice. (Note: removing a portfolio listing does not affect your ownership of the website in any way.)
4
Your Ongoing Costs (After Handoff)

Sagemind's work is completely free. After handoff, your only costs are the third-party services you control:

5
Content Deadline
  • All content (photos, business details, copy, logos, etc.) must be delivered within 30 days of signing.
  • If content is not received within 30 days, the project may be placed on hold until it arrives.
  • If content is not received within 60 days, Sagemind reserves the right to cancel this agreement. No hard feelings — life happens — but we need the material to do the work.
6
Revisions
  • 2 rounds of revisions are included at no charge.
  • A "round" means a consolidated set of feedback — please gather all your notes before submitting each round.
  • Additional revisions beyond the 2 included rounds are available at $75–150/hr, billed in 30-minute increments.
7
After Handoff
  • Sagemind delivers your website in fully working condition — tested and ready to go live.
  • After handoff, you are responsible for ongoing maintenance, updates, and any technical issues that arise.
  • Optional support is available from Sagemind on an hourly basis if you ever need help down the road. Just reach out.
