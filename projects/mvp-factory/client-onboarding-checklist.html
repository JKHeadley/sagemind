Welcome to the MVP Factory! 🎉

We're excited to build your website. This checklist walks you through everything you need to do from today through launch. We've designed the process to be as simple as possible — you handle the content and a couple of free accounts; we handle everything else.

The full process takes about 4–6 weeks once we have your content. Most of that time is us building and refining your site. Your biggest job right now is gathering your content and sending it to us within 30 days.

1

Content We Need From You

Complete within 30 days · Send to your project manager via email or Google Drive

This is the most important step. Check off each item as you gather it, then send everything together. Don't worry about perfection — we'll work with what you have.

  • ☐
    Business Name
    Exactly as you want it displayed on the website and in search results.
  • ☐
    Business Address
    Full address (street, city, state, zip). If you're home-based and prefer privacy, let us know and we'll handle it appropriately.
  • ☐
    Phone Number
    The number customers should use to reach you.
  • ☐
    Email Address (for contact form)
    When someone fills out your website's contact form, their message gets sent here. Can be any email you check regularly.
  • ☐
    Business Hours
    Days and hours you're open. Include any seasonal variations or closures if relevant.
  • ☐
    Logo
    High resolution preferred. PNG or SVG files work best. If you have multiple versions (dark/light background), send them all.
  • ☐
    Photos (at least 5–10)
    High-quality photos of your business, team, products, or services. Smartphone photos are fine as long as they're well-lit and in focus. More is better!
  • ☐
    Services / Menu List
    List each service or menu item with a short description. Include prices if you want them shown on the site (or let us know if you prefer "call for pricing").
  • ☐
    About Your Business
    1–3 paragraphs about your story, your mission, how long you've been in business, and what makes you different. Write it in your own voice — we'll polish it if needed.
  • ☐
    FAQ (5–10 Questions)
    Common questions your customers ask. Think: "Do you take appointments?", "What areas do you serve?", "How long does it take?", etc. Your AI chatbot will also use these.
  • ☐
    Social Media Links
    Instagram, Facebook, Yelp, Google Business, TikTok, or any other profiles you want linked on your site.
  • ☐
    Any Special Requests or Preferences
    Color preferences, examples of websites you like, features you want, anything you definitely don't want. The more you share, the better we can tailor the site to you.
  • ☐
    Google Business Profile Login (optional)
    If you have a Google Business Profile, share your login credentials securely. This lets us optimize your listing for better local search results — completely optional but recommended.
2

Accounts You Need to Create

Do this while we're building · These are yours — we never purchase anything on your behalf

You'll need to create three accounts yourself. Each one is straightforward, and we've written step-by-step instructions below. Important: We will never buy anything on your behalf. These accounts are yours to own and control.

~$10–15/year

🌐 Your Domain Name (e.g., yourbusiness.com)

Your domain is your website's address on the internet. You purchase and own it directly — we just help point it to your site.

  1. Go to namecheap.com or domains.google
  2. Type your business name in the search box and hit Search
  3. Pick your domain — a .com is strongly preferred (e.g., joesbakery.com)
  4. Add it to your cart and complete the purchase (usually $10–15/year)
  5. Create an account and save your login — you'll need it later

Once you have it, send us your domain name. We'll handle all the technical setup (DNS configuration) so it points to your website.

Free

☁️ Vercel Account (your website hosting)

Vercel is where your website will live. It's free, reliable, and used by millions of businesses. You don't need to do anything technical with it — we handle the setup.

  1. Go to vercel.com/signup
  2. Click "Continue with Email" and create an account
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Send us your Vercel login email — we'll connect your site to your account

The free tier (Hobby plan) is all you need. Your site will be fast, secure, and always on — at no monthly cost.

~$1–3/month

🤖 AI Chatbot API Key

Your site includes an AI-powered chatbot that answers customer questions 24/7. You sign up with an AI provider, and we build the chatbot. You pay the provider directly based on usage — typically a few dollars a month for a small business.

Choose one provider and sign up:

Provider Typical Cost Sign Up
OpenAI (ChatGPT) ~$2–3/mo platform.openai.com
Anthropic (Claude) ~$2–3/mo console.anthropic.com
Google AI (Gemini) ~$1–2/mo aistudio.google.com

Not sure which to pick? OpenAI or Anthropic are great choices for most businesses. Once you've signed up, generate an API key in your account and send it to us — we'll handle the rest.

3

Review & Revisions

~2 weeks after we receive your content · 2 revision rounds included

Once we have your content, we build your site and walk you through a structured review process. You'll have two full rounds to request changes — take your time and be thorough.

1

We Build Your Site (1–2 weeks)

We use your content to build a fully functional website with your real text, photos, branding, and AI chatbot. No placeholder lorem ipsum — your actual site.

2

Preview Link Sent to You

We send you a private preview link. Take your time reviewing every page. Write down everything you'd like changed — nothing is too small to mention.

3

Round 1 Revisions

Send us your feedback (list format is best). We make all requested changes and send an updated preview. This round is for the big stuff — layout, content, features.

4

Round 2 Revisions (Final)

Last chance for tweaks. This round is for fine-tuning — wording, photo choices, color adjustments, small additions. After this, we move to launch.

Additional revisions beyond 2 rounds are billed at $75–150/hour depending on the scope. To avoid extra charges, use your revision rounds thoroughly. Our goal is to get it right within the included rounds — we're on your side.
4

Launch & Handoff

Final 50% payment collected at launch · Everything you need to own and run your site

Once revisions are complete and the final payment is received, we go live. Here's exactly what happens and what you walk away owning.

What Happens at Launch

  • Site goes live on your domain
  • SSL certificate installed (https://)
  • AI chatbot activated on your API key
  • You receive a zip file with all your code & assets
  • You receive your documentation guide
  • We keep a permanent backup of your site
  • Final 50% payment collected via Stripe

What You Own

  • Complete website source code
  • All images, fonts, and design assets
  • SEO configuration and sitemap
  • AI chatbot codebase & configuration
  • Full documentation for making updates
  • No lock-in — move anywhere, anytime
Your site is truly yours. We don't retain any rights to your code or content. You can host it anywhere, modify it freely, or hire any developer to work on it in the future. We build it for you — you own it completely.
