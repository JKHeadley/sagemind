# Secret Rite of John the Apostle (A Speculative Take on The Book of Revelation as an Initiation Rite)



OVERVIEW OF STRUCTURE

Five Acts of Initiation

Each act corresponds to one phase of the Lukumí initiatory arc:

	1.	Separation / Purification – Leaving the profane world behind.

	2.	Crossroads / Trials – Facing internal and external conflicts.

	3.	Confrontation / Descent – Encountering the false self and worldly illusions.

	4.	Sacrifice / Resurrection – Death of ego and rebirth of true self.

	5.	Coronation / Return – Receiving the crown and the divine mission.



ACT I: SEPARATION / PURIFICATION

(Revelation 1–5)

*Theme: Awakening the Initiate*

	•	John’s Vision – The initiate receives the inner call through a spiritual crisis. The “Son of Man” among the lampstands is the Higher Self—terrible in appearance yet loving.

	•	Seven Churches – These are seven aspects of the psyche that must be purified. The evaluation of each church is the first purification and reckoning.

	•	The Throne and 24 Elders – The initiate’s inner circle, composed of the 12 Old Testament spies (representing ancestral karmic inheritance) and 12 Apostles (symbolizing potential spiritual mastery). This integration of the old and new reflects the Lukumí ritual of receiving ancestral wisdom alongside divine instruction.

	•	The Lion of Judah / Lamb with 7 Horns and 7 Eyes – The fusion of *Ali as the Lion of God* (courage, justice) with the *Imam-Lamb* who has mastered seven religious perspectives symbolizes the first sight of the “True Teacher” within. Seven eyes = gnosis, seven horns = power across traditions.

*In Lukumí terms: The initiate glimpses their tutelary orisha behind the veil.*

ACT II: CROSSROADS / TRIALS

(Revelation 6–9)

*Theme: Confronting Fate, Violence, Judgment, and Death*

This act is centered on the Four Horsemen, who each represent stages of karmic awakening and external challenge.

	•	White Horse (Ochosi / Muhammad’s Bow “Qaws al-Sa’diyya”) – Precision, law, and fate. The initiate is shown their destined path. Ochosi is the divine hunter whose arrow never misses—fate is now fixed, and destiny begins its unfolding.

	•	Red Horse (Oggun / Ali’s Sword “Zulfiqar”) – Symbolizes all the battles the initiate must fight, especially the inner wars. Oggun, blacksmith of fate, teaches struggle and endurance.

	•	Black Horse (Elegua / Cross) – Now the initiate reaches the crossroads. Elegua is the opener and closer of roads, just as the Lamb breaks open the seals. He weighs karma and gives the opportunity for self-correction.

	•	Pale Horse (Osun / Holy Grail) – Spiritual omens of death, illness, and psychological rupture. Osun is the silent observer of one’s head (destiny) and the bringer of warnings. The initiate now must discern the spiritual from the material.



Ancestors under the Tabernacle = 72 Martyrs of Ali

These martyrs serve as the foundation stones of the initiate’s new self. Their cries symbolize the unhealed trauma of one’s lineage and history. In Lukumí, they are like the otás de eggún in the sacred cauldron—witnesses of blood and memory.

ACT III: CONFRONTATION / DESCENT

(Revelation 10–13)

*Theme: Struggle with Worldly Powers and False Selves*

	       •     Virgin of the Apocalypse (Virgin of Fatima) – The initiate receives the “Hand of Fatima,” just as one receives the “Hand of Orula.” This is the full transmission of the divine mother’s protection, intuition, and wisdom.



	•	Beast of the Sea (False Messiah / Empire) – This is the imperial power structure the initiate once believed in. It represents all external governments, nations, systems that usurp divine sovereignty. It’s false *messianism*—the idea that salvation can come from a state.

	•	Beast of the Earth (False Prophet / Cultural Power) – This is the beast within: the religion, culture, education, and internalized identity that convinces people to serve empire in God’s name. It often resembles your own people—making it more deceptive.

	•	The Scarlet Beast (Foreign Pawns) – Marginalized peoples allowed to flow into the empire but never integrated. These groups become either scapegoats or weapons. They are symbolic of *shadow projections* and *sacrificial lambs*.

	•	The Whore of Babylon (Elite Education) – Babylon is not just a city—it’s the imperial university. She rides the beast and grooms the ruling class. This is the initiate’s former identity, groomed by the empire. It must be unmasked and renounced.

	•	Satan = Ignorance / Agent Provocateur – Satan is not a monster but the *psychological process of sabotage*. It’s the provocateur who lures the initiate into discrediting themselves. Symbolically, it’s reactive self-destruction, born of unhealed wounds.



ACT IV: SACRIFICE / RESURRECTION

(Revelation 14–19)

*Theme: Rebirth Through Fire and Judgment*

	•	144,000 = Crown Chakra / Crown of Ifá – Those who are “sealed” with divine knowledge. The initiate reaches the sahasrāra—the lotus of 144,000 petals. In Lukumí, this is the moment the initiate “receives the crown” and is crowned with Orisha consciousness.

	•	Harvest of the Earth – The old self and its karmic fruits are harvested. The “winepress of wrath” is the burning of impure attachments.

	•	Fall of Babylon – The ego structure tied to imperial consciousness collapses. The Whore is stripped, the empire mocked, and the false religion cast into fire.

	•	Marriage of the Lamb – The initiate is now wed to their higher self, the Lamb. In Lukumí, this is like entering full initiation, becoming a child of the orisha.

	•	Return of the Rider (True Messiah) – Christ on the White Horse now *returns* as the sword of divine truth. This is the resurrected self, no longer passive but active in divine justice.



ACT V: CORONATION / RETURN

(Revelation 20–22)

*Theme: Divine Authority and World Renewal*

	•	Millennium and Final Battle – The initiate rules for a symbolic “1000 years” (full maturity) before the last test arises.

	•	Gog and Magog = Al-Qaeda / Duality – The final internal battle: the seductive lure of binary thinking, extremism, and spiritual fanaticism. They are allowed to rise as the last shadow to be faced.

	•	Final Judgment – Now the initiate stands fully integrated. Every part of their psyche (the Book of Life) is read. All internalized empires and false gods are cast away.

	•	New Jerusalem – The soul becomes the Holy City. Not a place, but a state of consciousness where God dwells inside. The Tree of Life and Water of Life appear, completing the mystical circuit.



FINAL INITIATIC MESSAGE

The *Book of Revelation* is not about doom—it’s about transformation.

It is an apocalypse in the truest sense: an unveiling of the divine within.

The initiate, through trials and tribulations, must pierce through empire, ego, war, cultural delusion, and death itself—ultimately to stand reborn, crowned, and awakened. Just as in Lukumí, the initiate becomes a vessel for divine forces—not as a passive believer, but as a conscious actor in the cosmic drama.
