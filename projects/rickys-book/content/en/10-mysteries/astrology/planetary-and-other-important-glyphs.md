# Planetary and Other Important Glyphs



1. The Sun ☉

	•	Glyph: A circle with a central dot.

	•	Symbolism: The circle represents wholeness or the spirit; the dot is the seed of consciousness—the divine spark.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Ego, identity, life force, father, vitality, purpose.



2. The Moon ☽

	•	Glyph: A crescent shape.

	•	Symbolism: Receptivity, cycles, the soul’s inner world. The crescent is the partial light of the Sun (reflected).

	•	Astrological Meaning: Emotions, instincts, memory, mother, habits, subconscious.



3. Mercury ☿

	•	Glyph: A circle (spirit) topped with a crescent (soul), sitting on a cross (matter).

	•	Symbolism: Union of soul, spirit, and matter; also resembles the caduceus.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Communication, intellect, coordination, commerce, trickster.



4. Venus ♀

	•	Glyph: A circle (spirit) over a cross (matter).

	•	Symbolism: Spirit descending into matter; harmony and beauty.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Love, beauty, values, receptivity, femininity, attraction.



5. Mars ♂

	•	Glyph: A circle (spirit) with an arrow pointing outward and up (action).

	•	Symbolism: Directed force; masculine energy.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Drive, aggression, sexuality, war, initiative, masculinity.



6. Jupiter ♃

	•	Glyph: A crescent (soul) attached to a cross (matter).

	•	Symbolism: Soul rising over material existence; spiritual growth.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Expansion, luck, philosophy, religion, abundance.



7. Saturn ♄

	•	Glyph: A cross (matter) above a crescent (soul).

	•	Symbolism: Matter suppressing the soul; limitation; karma.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Discipline, restriction, time, karma, authority, maturity.



8. Uranus ♅

	•	Glyph: A combination of the Mars and Sun glyphs, sometimes shown as an H with a globe.

	•	Symbolism: Originality, sudden change, higher mind.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Innovation, rebellion, shocks, higher consciousness.



9. Neptune ♆

	•	Glyph: A trident, resembling the weapon of Poseidon.

	•	Symbolism: Spirit (circle) pierced by soul (crescent) in a watery realm.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Illusion, dreams, mysticism, dissolution, compassion.



10. Pluto ♇ or 𓂀

	•	Glyph: A circle (spirit) over a crescent (soul) over a cross (matter); or sometimes “P” + “L” (for Percival Lowell).

	•	Symbolism: Complete transformation; the alchemical process.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Power, transformation, death/rebirth, subconscious.



Other Important Glyphs

Chiron ⚷

	•	Glyph: A key-like symbol.

	•	Symbolism: Key to healing; wounded healer.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Deep wounds and healing, where pain becomes medicine.



Lilith (Black Moon Lilith) ⚸

	•	Glyph: A crescent above a cross (like an inverted Venus).

	•	Symbolism: The shadow feminine, primal feminine force.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Raw sexuality, rejection, independence, rebellion.



The Ascendant (Rising Sign) AC

	•	Glyph: Not standardized, often just “AC” or a rising sun symbol.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Persona, first impressions, filter of consciousness.



Midheaven (MC)

	•	Glyph: Also just “MC.”

	•	Astrological Meaning: Career, public image, destiny point.



North Node ☊ / South Node ☋

	•	Glyphs: Horseshoe shapes—North Node open up, South Node open down.

	•	Symbolism: Points of destiny (nodes of the Moon’s orbit).

	•	Astrological Meaning: Life path (NN) vs karmic past (SN).



Part of Fortune ⊗

	•	Glyph: A circle with an “X” in it.

	•	Symbolism: Where spirit and matter intersect favorably.

	•	Astrological Meaning: Joy, prosperity, natural talents.
