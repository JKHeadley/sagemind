# Natal Chart in Western Astrology



The Natal Chart should be viewed from a perspective outside of the celestial sphere looking through the constellation Octans with Middle heaven placed at the bottom and middle earth at the top, ascending at the right side and descending at the left. All celestial objects move clockwise around the chart.
