# Review of Solomonic Inspired Initiations Preserved within York Rite Freemasonry (Cryptic Masonry: Council Degrees)



Cryptic Masonry focuses on the preservation of Masonic secrets and deeper spiritual themes.

# 1. Royal Master (8°) — Theme: The transmission of wisdom and preparation for death.
# Ritual & Ceremony:

The candidate meets Hiram Abiff shortly before his death and learns the importance of preserving wisdom for future generations.

Key Events:

- A dialogue with Hiram Abiff about mortality.
- A warning about the impending loss of the Master’s Word.



Symbols:

- Broken Column: Represents Hiram’s death and the unfinished nature of human endeavors.



Moral Lesson:

- Wisdom is eternal and must be safeguarded for future generations.



# 2. Select Master (9°) — Theme: The construction of the Secret Vault.
# Ritual & Ceremony:

The candidate plays the role of one of the chosen workmen tasked with concealing sacred treasures beneath the Temple.

Symbols:

- Secret Vault: Represents hidden wisdom.
- Triangle: A symbol of divine knowledge.



Moral Lesson:

- Sacred knowledge must be protected from corruption.



# 3. Super Excellent Master (10°) (Optional Degree) — Theme: The consequences of failing to uphold Masonic principles.
# Ritual & Ceremony:

The candidate symbolically experiences the destruction of Jerusalem and learns about the dangers of spiritual neglect.

Moral Lesson:

- Disobedience to divine law leads to downfall.
